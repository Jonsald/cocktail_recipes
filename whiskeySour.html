Whiskey Sour

Home

Description :

A Whiskey Sour is a classic and well-balanced cocktail known for its refreshing, tangy-sweet flavor.
It combines the rich warmth of whiskey with the bright acidity of lemon juice and a touch of sweetness,
often smoothed out with the optional addition of egg white for a silky texture.

Ingredients :

  • 2 oz bourbon or whiskey
  • 3/4 oz fresh lemon juice
  • 1/2 oz simple syrup
  • (Optional: 1 egg white for a silky texture)

Instructions :

  1. Shake everything with ice.
  2. Strain into a glass with fresh ice.
  3. Garnish with a cherry or orange slice.
