Description :

A Vodka Tonic is a light, crisp, and refreshing cocktail that combines
the clean, neutral flavor of vodka with the slightly bitter, effervescent
bite of tonic water. Itâ€™s a simple two-ingredient drink that is quick to
make and easy to enjoy,often served over ice with a wedge of lime for a citrusy twist.

Ingredients :

  • 2oz vodka
  • 4-6 oz tonic water (adjust taste)
  • Lime wedge (for garnish)
  • Ice

Instructions :

  1. Fill a highball glass with ice
  2. Pour in the vodka
  3. Top off with tonic water
  4. Stir gently
  5. Squeeze in a lime wedge and drop it in
